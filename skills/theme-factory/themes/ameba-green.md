# Ameba Green

The official CyberAgent/Ameba brand theme featuring the signature green identity.

## Color Palette

- **Ameba Green**: `#2d8c3c` - Primary brand color
- **Green Light**: `#e7f5e9` - Backgrounds and cards
- **Yellow Green**: `#82be28` - Accent highlights
- **Gray 100**: `#08121a` - Primary text
- **White**: `#ffffff` - Slide backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headers**: Meiryo (Bold)
- **Body Text**: Meiryo

## Best Used For

Official CyberAgent materials, Ameba products, corporate communications, brand presentations.
