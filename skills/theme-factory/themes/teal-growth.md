# Teal Growth

A fresh, modern theme using Spindle's Expressive Green (Teal) palette.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Teal**: `#003d2c` - Primary accent
- **Mint Fresh**: `#e0f5f1` - Soft backgrounds
- **Growth Teal**: `#00a688` - Interactive highlights
- **Gray 100**: `#08121a` - Primary text
- **White**: `#ffffff` - Slide backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headers**: Meiryo (Bold)
- **Body Text**: Meiryo

## Best Used For

Health and wellness, sustainability initiatives, growth metrics, environmental content.
