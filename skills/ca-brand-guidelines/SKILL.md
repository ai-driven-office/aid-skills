## Design Principles

1. **Green as identity**: Ameba Green (`#2d8c3c`) anchors all materials
2. **Light over dark**: Prefer white backgrounds with green accents
3. **4px grid**: Spacing at 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32, 40, 48px
4. **Rounded corners**: Consistent border-radius for friendly feel
