|------|--------|----------|
| `logo_CyberAgent_01_RGB.png` | PNG | Web, digital |
| `logo_CyberAgent_01_Wh_RGB.png` | PNG | Light backgrounds |
| `logo_CyberAgent_02_RGB.png` | PNG | Alternate variant |
| `logo_CyberAgent_02_Wh_RGB.png` | PNG | Alternate (light bg) |

| `logo_CyberAgent_CMYK.ai` | AI | Print materials |

| `logo_CyberAgentGroup_CMYK.ai` | AI | Group (print) |
